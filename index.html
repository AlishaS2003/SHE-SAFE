    About

    Your Safety is Our Priority

    Empowering women through knowledge, community, and support

    • Our Vision
    • Our Mission
    • Our Story

    Our vision is to create a world where every woman feels safe, respected, and empowered to thrive. We envision a society free from violence and discrimination, where women can pursue their dreams without fear.

    A Safe Environment

    We aim to foster safe spaces for women in all aspects of life, including public areas, workplaces, and online platforms, ensuring that every woman can navigate her world confidently.

    Empowered Women

    We believe in empowering women through education, awareness, and resources that help them understand their rights and responsibilities, enabling them to stand up for themselves and others.

    Community Engagement

    We envision a strong network of engaged individuals and organizations that work collaboratively to promote women's safety and create lasting change in our communities.

    Our mission is to empower women by providing accessible resources and fostering a supportive community focused on safety. We believe that knowledge is power, and through our platform, we aim to equip women with the tools they need to protect themselves and assert their rights confidently.

    Safety Education

    We offer comprehensive educational resources that cover self-defense, situational awareness, and legal rights, ensuring that women have the knowledge they need to navigate their environments safely.

    Community Support

    We foster a vibrant community where women can share their experiences, seek advice, and support one another. By building connections, we strengthen our collective resolve to promote safety and well-being.

    Advocacy for Change

    We advocate for policies and initiatives that promote women's safety at local and national levels. By partnering with organizations and community leaders, we strive to create a safer society for all women.

    Founded by a group of passionate women who experienced firsthand the challenges of navigating safety in everyday life, SheSafe was born out of a desire to create positive change. We understand that safety is not just a personal issue; it’s a societal one. By coming together, we can challenge norms, share knowledge, and create an environment where women can thrive.

    Our Journey

    Our journey began with the recognition of the pressing need for a dedicated platform focused on women's safety. We have since grown into a community of advocates and supporters committed to making a difference.

    Collective Strength

    We believe in the power of collective strength. By sharing our stories and experiences, we can inspire others and create a ripple effect of change that extends far beyond our individual lives.

    Our Commitment

    Our commitment is to continue advocating for women's rights and safety, ensuring that every woman has access to the resources and support she needs to lead a safe and empowered life.

    About Alisha

    Alisha is a visionary leader with a decade of experience in the tech industry and a passion for leveraging technology for social good. As the CEO of SheSafe, she has made it her mission to create a safer world for women, bringing together innovative ideas, cutting-edge technology, and a compassionate approach.

    Her journey started in her early days in Chennai, where she saw firsthand the challenges women face regarding personal safety and empowerment. Driven by these experiences, she pursued a career in technology and eventually built a company dedicated to addressing these issues on a larger scale.

    Under Alisha’s leadership, SheSafe has grown from a small startup to a recognized name in women’s safety advocacy. Her innovative spirit has led to the development of groundbreaking safety tools and resources, and her strategic thinking has helped SheSafe forge partnerships with influential organizations worldwide.

    Alisha’s dedication to SheSafe is not just about creating products but also about building a movement. She envisions a world where women can live confidently, and she is committed to making this vision a reality. Alisha’s philosophy is simple: every woman deserves to feel safe and empowered, no matter where she is or what challenges she faces.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Your safety is our priority. Find answers to your questions about our services.

    What is the Basic Safety Plan?

    The Basic Safety Plan is a free service that provides access to emergency helplines, safety tips, and a supportive community network.

    What features does the Premium Safety Plan include?

    The Premium Safety Plan includes all Basic Plan features, access to personal safety devices, monthly self-defense workshops, and an emergency contact alert system.

    How can I access self-defense training?

    Self-defense training sessions are available to Premium and Ultimate plan subscribers, and workshops are conducted monthly for hands-on practice.

    What is the Ultimate Safety Plan?

    The Ultimate Safety Plan offers all features from the Premium Plan plus exclusive training videos, one-on-one safety consultations, and personalized safety assessments.

    Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

    Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Simply log into your account and follow the cancellation process outlined in your account settings.
